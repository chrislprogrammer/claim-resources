GAF Documents & Guidelines

Here are different GAF documents and guidelines you may be looking for!

Visit GAF for more information about their products.

Timberline HDZ Installation Instructions

Installation guidelines for Timberline HDZ.

Felt Overlap Bulletin

The official stance from GAF on properly overlaying hips and ridges with felt.

Hail Damage Bulletin

GAF's official stance on what is considered hail damage to their shingles.

Replacement of Storm Damaged Shingles Bulletin

GAF answers some important questions in this document:

  • What are some causes for storm damage to shingles?
  • What are examples of latent damage?
  • What if I have granules loss concerns?

Acceptable Substrate For GAF Shingles

GAF's official statement on what is acceptable substrate for their shingles.

Do Not Mix Letter

GAF's official letter stating to not mix new and old shingles because of granule sourcing and manufacture changes

Do Not Mix Old and New Timberline/ Prestique

This letter says not to mix the old and new Timberline/Prestique Shingles.

GAF and Elk Merge Letter #1

This letter confirms that GAF merged with Elk in 2007 and that Elk shingles are no longer in production.

GAF and Elk Merge Letter #2

This is another letter from GAF confirming the merger and to not mix shingles.

GAF Timberline English Size Shingles Discontinued

GAF's official letter saying the English size of Timberline shingles have been phased out.

Do Not Mix Metric and English

GAF's official letter stating to not mix metric and english measurement shingles.

Country Mansion II Discontinuation Letter

GAF's official letter stating that Country Mansion II Shingles have been discontinued.

GAF Sentinel Discontinuation Letter

GAF's official letter stating that the GAF Sentinel shingles have been discontinued.

Timberline Prestique 40 HD Discontinued Letter

GAF's official letter stating that the GAF Timberline Prestique 40 High Definition shingles have been discontinued.

Timberline Prestique 30 Discontinued Letter

GAF's official letter stating that the GAF Timberline Prestique 30 shingles have been discontinued.

GAF Timberline HD Discontinued Letter

GAF's official letter stating that the GAF Timberline HD shingles have been discontinued.

GAF Disco Heather Blend Discontinued Letter

GAF's official letter stating that the GAF Disco Heather Blend Color shingles have been discontinued.

GAF Timberline Natural Shadow D.N.M.

GAF's official letter stating that the GAF Timberline Natural Shadow shingles should not be mixed with Timberline HD/HDZ.

Royal Sovereign Do Not Mix

GAF's official letter stating that the old GAF Royal Sovereign shingles should not be mixed with the newer Royal Sovereign Shingles.

Royal Sovereign New Source

GAF's official letter stating that in 2014 the Royal Sovereign Shingles source changed from Minneapolis to Mount Vernon
