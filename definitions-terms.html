Insurance Terms & Defintions

Term Definition
Actual Cash Value (ACV) Actual cash value (ACV) is the amount equal to the replacement cost minus depreciation of a damaged or stolen property at the time of the loss.
Replacement Cost Value (RCV) The cost to replace the damaged property at todays pricing.
